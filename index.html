About Me Hello! I’m an Aspiring Data Scientist transitioning from a career in logistics and procurement management to the dynamic field of data science.

Get to know me!

Hi, I’m Vasilis Katsikas, an aspiring Data Scientist with a unique mix of experience in procurement management, supply chain operations, and a background in the military. Based in Athens, Greece, I’ve managed large-scale projects, streamlined logistics workflows, and led teams to success under pressure.

My time in the military taught me discipline, resilience, and the value of working as a team—qualities that I carry with me into every challenge I take on. Now, I’m channeling that same determination into my journey in data science.

I’m currently sharpening my skills through a Data Science Bootcamp at Big Blue Data Academy, where I’m learning everything from data cleaning and exploratory data analysis to machine learning and data visualization. It’s hands-on, challenging, and incredibly rewarding.

My go-to tools include Python, Pandas, SQL, web scraping, Power BI, and Excel. I’ve used them to tackle projects like analyzing sales trends, improving logistics operations, and building dashboards that turn raw data into meaningful insights.

I’m excited to connect with others who share a passion for solving real-world problems with data. Let’s explore how we can make an impact together!

PAOK FC Player Performance Analysis(Web Scraping and PowerBI)

This project involves web scraping data from the official PAOK FC site, analyzing player performance using Python (Pandas & Matplotlib) , and visualizing insights in Power BI. It covers key areas such as goals, assists, accuracy, and defensive actions. Skills demonstrated include web scraping with BeautifulSoup, data manipulation with Pandas, and creating advanced visualizations in Power BI using DAX formulas for interactive dashboards. The project highlights my ability to transform raw data into actionable insights and present them in a user-friendly manner.
